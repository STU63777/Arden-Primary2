After School Clubs

Last month we started a scheme for after school clubs to help keep the children entertained; while they wait for their parents to arrive and take them home. We have an art club, maths club and sports club in place to make sure that the kids can still learn while they wait. We do understand that parents can be busy with so we thought we would give back by starting these clubs.
